At Zyra, we believe fragrance is powerful. It’s an invisible force that envelops you, transforms your mood, and tells your story. Inspired by Middle Eastern culture, our fragrances celebrate femininity, confidence, and luxury.

Our Founder’s Passion

Evocative Arabian Nights Inspiration

The inspiration behind Zyra comes from the mysteries of Arabia—incense-filled nights, blooming florals, rich spices, and golden warmth. Each fragrance reflects heritage, empowerment, and bold self-expression.

Honoring Heritage with Authentic Craftsmanship

Designed in France and inspired by Middle Eastern traditions, Zyra fragrances are crafted using the finest ethically sourced ingredients. From Turkish rose to Moroccan jasmine and Oud wood, every note tells a story of luxury and depth.
